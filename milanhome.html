Milan

  • Sci-Tech Events
  • Cultural Events
  • Sports Events

"Milan" is the annual techno-cultural-sports general championship of IIT Hyderabad. It consists of 10 Sports, 15 Cultural and 6 technical events conducted between all the hostel blocks. In this first edition of Milan, the sports competitions will start from 20th February, Cultural events from 25th February and the Technical events from 21st February. The closing ceremony includes including two pronites- Sufi Night by MS Nizami Brothers on 29th February 2020 and an EDM night on 1st March 2020.
Follow our social media handles to stay updated with all the latest announcements: Facebook,Instagram. Log on to Discourse for a discussion platform to voice your views!
